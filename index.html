Calorie Mate

How to use

Have you ever tried to work out the calories contained in a specific portion size and found it tricky?

This app is designed to help!

First, enter the pack size in grams. Next, enter the calorie example shown on the pack (usually this is something like 135kcal per 100g).

Lastly, enter your specific portion size (grams) and hit Calculate. The app will then show how may calories are contained in your specific portion.

Simple!
